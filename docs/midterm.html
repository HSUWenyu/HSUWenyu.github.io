Introduction

Total area: 163.4 square kilometers

Population: 2.817 million

Taichung City is located in the center of Taiwan. With a dense transportation network from north to south and an important base for direct cross-strait flights in the future, Taichung City is located at the most important hub and has become the "Heart of Taiwan".

The characteristic, comfortable and pleasant living environment, with lower housing prices than the north, make Taichung City have a complete living function. Coupled with the abundant education and human resources gathered from 12 colleges and universities, Taichung City ranks second in the country in terms of urban competitiveness.

Diversity and innovation are the source of competitiveness of Taichung, a consumer city; the service industry is the most important industry in Taichung City.

Geography

Taichung City is located in the Taichung Basin along the main western coastal plain that stretches along the west coast from northern Taiwan almost to the southern tip.

The city borders Changhua County, Nantou County, Hualien County, Yilan County, Hsinchu County and Miaoli County.

Taichung consists of 29 districts, 28 districts and 1 mountain indigenous district.

Climate

Taichung has a warm humid subtropical climate bordering on a tropical monsoon climate.

Due to the protection provided by the Central Mountain Range to the east and the Miaoli hills to the north, Taichung is rarely severely affected by typhoons.

Taichung is sunny all year round and the climate is pleasant. The average area of green space owned by citizens is the first in Taiwan, and it has been voted as the "Best Place to Live"
