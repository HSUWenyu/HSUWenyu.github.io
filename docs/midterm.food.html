  • Suncake
    •

    The typical fillings consist of maltose (condensed malt sugar). Suncakes are round, and they may vary in size. Most people eat them with tea, and some people dissolve them in hot water to make a porridge-like dessert.




  • Dajia's Taro ball
    •

    Dajia is famous for its taro. The taro planted in Dajia is even softer. Dajia Taro has also developed numerous food products, such as beverages, ice products, and hot pot ingredients. One of these is taro ball.




  • Miyahara(Ice cream)
    •

    Miyachara is an important landmark and scenic spot in Taichung. It was originally an eye hospital established by the Japanese. It is a model of commercial operation of historical buildings. The characteristic of Miyahara ice cream is that you can choose an ice cream shell that is as big as a palm, choose the flavor you want to eat, and then choose a variety of biscuits, chocolate, cranberry, cheesecake... and other different ingredients for matching.
