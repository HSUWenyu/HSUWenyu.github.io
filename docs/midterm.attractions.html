ATTRACTIONS

  • Rainbow Village

    • Ranbow Village is a military dependents' village that was converted into street art.As residents painted on the old house to be demolished, and triggered others to launch the "Rescue Rainbow Village" activity, the Taichung City Government rezoned the place as a park and preserved it




  • National Taichung Theater

    • The opera house has the first double stage stage in Taiwan. It has a large theater (2,007 seats), a medium theater (796 seats), a small theater (200 seats) and a small outdoor theater, as well as dining spaces and sky gardens.




  • Gaomei Wetlands

    • Gaomei Wetlands is an ecological reserve on the west coast of Taiwan. It is the coastal wetlands formed by comprehensive fresh water injection and tidal alternation.
